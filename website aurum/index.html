AURUM Microfinance

Ang AURUM Microfinance ay isang Non Government Organization (NGO)
na ang layunin ay makatulong at mapataas ang antas ng kabuhayan ng
ating mga miyembro. We advocate to strenghten financial knowledge and
wellness. AURUM MICROFINANCE CORPORATION is dedicated to the
highest quality of financial and non-financial services delivered with
strength, transparency, honesty and people oriented service. Passionate
about helping our customers of today and the generation to come.
